About Me

Hello, My name is Tayras. I have been developing things for over 5 years, where I first started learning how to code my own discord bots. The first year coding discord bots what very difficult and frustrating, with my bots only being able to preform the simplist task, as well as none of my bots has 24/7 hosting at the time. Although through time and effort i've progressed far where to now, almost 6 years later, i can confidently say that my bots are some of the best around. I took a photoshop and graphic design class for 3 years, including an extra year of experience where I was self-taught. I also started off making roblox games with friends which didn't really get anywhere, until I started learning how to 3d model back in 2021 which helped gain me recognition among the roblox game development community, where I was hired onto my first project which was a GAR border/roleplay type game. Later my friend reached out to me and asked him to help out with this game him and someone he knew (DunGufed) were buying soon and I agreed, this game later became my biggest success as of now. Me and DunGufed have progressed far together and started our own game studio called BlueRain Development, which we plan to continue to pursue and improve upon.
